Penguin Slide is a fun, fast-paced **endless runner game** set in the icy South Pole, where players control penguins as they dodge obstacles and collect coins. It's a perfect game for anyone who loves penguins, snow, and some good old-fashioned competitive fun!

## Features
- **Multiplayer Mode**: Race with a friend! Controls for both players.
- **Online Leaderboard**: Compete with players globally and see who ranks #1.
- **Power-Ups**: Collect shields and speed boosts to help you run further.
- **Boss Mode**: Face the Blizzard Boss after scoring 50 points.
- **Penguin Skins**: Unlock and use different penguin skins with special powers.
- **Daily Rewards**: Collect rewards each day you play.
- **Achievements**: Earn achievements for completing in-game challenges.
- **Seasonal Events**: Special events with exclusive skins and power-ups.

## How to Play
1. Use the **arrow keys** to move your penguin:
   - **Arrow Left**: Move left.
   - **Arrow Right**: Move right.
   - **Arrow Up**: Jump.
2. **Collect coins** while avoiding obstacles and enemies.
3. **Activate power-ups** like shields and speed boosts.
4. Try to get the highest score and **unlock new penguin skins**!
5. Compete with your friends on the **leaderboard**!
